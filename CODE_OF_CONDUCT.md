# Code of Conduct

ScriptCut contributors are expected to keep discussions respectful, specific, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume good intent while asking for evidence when needed.
- Keep feedback tied to code, docs, design, or user impact.
- Respect that users may be working with private local media.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Publishing private user media, credentials, or logs
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments

Maintainers may moderate issues, discussions, pull requests, or contributors that violate these expectations.
